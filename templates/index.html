Facial Liveness Demo

Face liveness detection is a security technology designed to distinguish between real human faces and fake representations such as photos, videos, or masks. It is a crucial component in biometric authentication systems that aims to ensure that the individual being verified is genuinely present and not using spoofing techniques.

Please follow the following instructions as we proceed forward:

  1. Ensure your face is well-lit and visible to the camera.
  2. Position yourself in front of the camera.
  3. Click the Open Webcam button to begin the liveness check.
